Virtual Environment Project

Research / Design / Development

The theme of this project is “Learn to paint with Bob Ross”. Within the application, we visualize the participant standing next to Bob Ross while he teaches you how to paint. The application will have multiple modes. In one of them, the participants will be instructed to follow along with Bob Ross -- this mode will be instructional and will aim at teaching the participants the basic techniques. In another mode, the participant will have the ability to freestyle and Bob Ross will provide companionship and encouragement.

We will use Unity and C# to create the virtual environment.
