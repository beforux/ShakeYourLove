order in sound - sound mapping
The Goldberg Variations, BWV 988, is a musical composition for harpsichord by Johann Sebastian Bach, consisting of an aria and a set of 30 variations. It shows the order of the universe. This project use the processing to visualize the music into 2D, or even into 3D to explore the underlying order of Goldberg Variations.
